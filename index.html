How It Works

Using our nasal clip is effortless and effective. Follow these steps to enjoy better airflow and relief from snoring or congestion.

Clean Step 1

Clean your nose thoroughly to remove dirt/oil. Let your nose dry completely before applying.
Pro-tip: Use soap + water or an alcohol prep pad.

Apply Step 2

Using the applicator, apply one tab to each side of your nose. Press each tab to your skin.

Tab placement Step 3

- Place steel discs directly above the nostril
- Ensure 100% contact with skin on nose (not cheeks)
- Tabs must be placed correctly for the band to attach.

Attach Step 4

Snap the U-will band onto the tabs.
Pro-tip: Ensure magnets face down.
